Bocken can only be used by UTN, the sections at TekNat and UTN:s associated organisations!

In order to drive Bocken you need to sign an agreement. Follow these steps:

  1. Download the agreement
  2. Read through the agreement
  3. Fill in the form and sign it. You can do this digitally on www.digisigner.com/free-electronic-signature. Press on DONE and then on download when you have signed it to download it.
  4. Send the agreement along with a picture of your driving licens to {{ klubbmastare_email }}
